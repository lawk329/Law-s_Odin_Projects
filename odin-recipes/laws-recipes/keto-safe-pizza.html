Law's Keto Safe Pizza

A low carbohydrate, high fiber, delicious alternative to store bought high carbohydrate pizza.

Ingredients

  • #1 & 1/2 cups luke warm water.
  • #4 tablespoons olive oil.
  • #1 box King Arthur Keto Pizza Crust mix.
  • 1/4 pound italian sausage.
  • 1/4 pound chorizo, if desired.
  • #1 grass-fed beef burger
  • #1 & 1/2 small jars of pizza sauce.
  • 1/2 lb. of your choice of cheese.
  • Pizza seasoning.

Cooking Instructions

This makes about qty.8, 4"x 6" slices of pizza.

  1. Preheat the oven to 340 degrees.
  2. Begin with prepping the dough per the box instructions.
  3. Melt and spread butter on 11"x 17" baking sheet.
  4. Roll out dough evenly across sheet, coat hands w/ butter to ease the sticking.
  5. Add the pizza sauce to the top.
  6. Add the cheese.
  7. Add the meats, place as desired.
  8. Add the seasoning evenly across the pizza.
  9. Cook until cheese is golden brown about 35-40 minutes.
  10. Remove from oven and let stand 5-10 minutes.
  11. Cut into slices and enjoy!
