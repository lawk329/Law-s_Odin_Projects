Law's Healthy Salad

A very well rounded salad that feeds the body, brain, and gut!

Ingredients

  • The One Bean soy bean fettucini.
  • Spring mix and Spinach
  • Onions
  • Tomatoes
  • Sauerkraut
  • Additional vegetables of choice.
  • Berries of your choice
  • Olive oil
  • Dressing of your choice

Prep Instructions

This makes about one large bowl of salad that can serve as a meal.

  1. Prep the pasta per the box instructions.
  2. Lay the pasta in the bed of the bowl, add oil.
  3. Add the lettuces.
  4. Add the remaining ingredients.
  5. Top off with a touch of your favorite dressing.
  6. Enjoy!
