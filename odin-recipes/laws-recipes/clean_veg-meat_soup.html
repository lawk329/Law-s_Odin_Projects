Law's Clean Veg and Meat Soup

A cleaner, fresher, and lower sodium soup than store bought canned.

Ingredients

  • #3 cups or quarts of chicken or vegetable broth.
  • #3 cups or quarts of water.
  • #2 chicken breasts.
  • 1/2 to 1 lb. grass-fed beef.
  • #1 cup mixed or beans of choice.
  • #3 cups vegetables of choice.
  • Add rice if desired.

Cooking Instructions

This makes about 12 qts. of soup, so adjust the quantities as desired. Add the broth and water into a 20 qt. soup kettle. Add the hardest and longest to cook ingredients first.

  1. Begin cooking the beans. Bring the mix to a boil, then simmer covered for 30 minutes.
  2. Add the rice if desired, maintain a simmer.
  3. Add fresh or frozen vegetables.
  4. Add seasoning to taste: Mild salt, pepper, etc.
  5. Chop up, separately half cook and add in the chicken pieces.
  6. Separately cook to medium, break up in pieces and add in the beef.
  7. Add pasta last. It will soften slowly. Adding too soon will cause it to shred.
  8. Season to taste with pepper, a little salt, etc.
  9. Allow to cool before storing in refrigerator.
